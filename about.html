About Clipzy

Clipzy is a video hosting app designed specifically for editors. Our platform is built by a team of over 60 developers, animators, legal advisors, beta testers, and more.

Powered by Electron with Auth0 integration for secure sign-up, Clipzy aims to revolutionize the way editors share and collaborate on video content.
